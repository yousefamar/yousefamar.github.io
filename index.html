About

Software, hardware, and everything in between!

As an MEng graduate in Computer Systems Engineering with Business Management, my skills are multi-faceted. With strengths in hardware design, low to high-level programming, and every layer of web development, but also project management and business administration, I am a jack of all trades.

I live to create and empower others to do the same. I care about technology (obviously), education, and creative expression, among other things too.

I am currently writing up my PhD thesis on Systems, Networks, Privacy, and Security. As my PhD grant nears its end, I am on the market for short-term, part-time employment while I finish my PhD.
